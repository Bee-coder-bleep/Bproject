Privacy Policy

How Bonfire Adventures collects, uses, and protects your information.

Last updated: July 2026

1. Who we are

Bonfire Adventures is a Kenya-based tour operator offering safari, beach, and international travel packages. This policy explains how we handle personal information collected through our website, contact forms, and WhatsApp communications.

2. Information we collect

  • Contact details you provide voluntarily, such as your name, phone number, and email address, when you fill in our contact form or message us on WhatsApp.
  • Travel preferences you share with us, such as destinations, dates, group size, and budget, to help us prepare a quote or itinerary.
  • Basic technical information collected automatically by our hosting and analytics tools, such as browser type and pages visited, to help us understand site usage.

3. How we use your information

  • To respond to your enquiries and prepare travel quotes or bookings.
  • To communicate with you about your trip via phone, email, or WhatsApp.
  • To improve our website and the packages we offer.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share necessary booking details (such as your name and travel dates) with hotels, airlines, or partner operators solely to fulfil a booking you have requested.

4. WhatsApp communication

When you click a "Chat on WhatsApp" button on our site, you are taken to WhatsApp's own platform, which has its own privacy practices. Messages you send us are used only to respond to your enquiry and are not used for unsolicited marketing.

5. Data retention

We keep enquiry and booking information only for as long as needed to provide our services and meet our legal or accounting obligations.

6. Your rights

Under the Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019, you have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data held by us. To make a request, contact us using the details on our Contact page.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.

8. Contact us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, reach out via the phone numbers or WhatsApp link on our Contact page, or visit us at Yala Towers, 4th Floor, Junction of Koinange & Biashara St, Nairobi.
